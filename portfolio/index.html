News Paper Website

More news, less clutter.

The News Paper is a website layout showcases a beautiful and eye-catching headline section above the fold. At the middle, it offers you a tile section where you can post different news feeds, news categories, announcements and more.

Combining the technolgoy of both floats and flexbox, this layout can be viewed on mini-laptops, tablets, smartphones or even on a Samsung Fold. Amaaaazing!

Minimalist E-Commerce

Product-focused, more sales.

The Minimalist E-Commerce design boasts both elegance and simplicity. Developed with the latest technologies, this layout is fast and user-friendly.

Thanks floats and flexbox technologies, together with Media Query, this e-commerce website layout is responsive to adjust on all screen sizes.

With the services/ products sorted on a beautiful UI cards, Minimalist E-Commerce helps your customer to focus more on what matters most -- product benefits.
